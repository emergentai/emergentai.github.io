We're an Approved Government of Canada AI Supplier

Emergent AI has been approved as a responsible AI supplier by the Government of Canada.

We're grateful to share that Emergent AI has been approved as a supplier on the Government of Canada's list of interested AI suppliers for responsible AI services.

This is a meaningful milestone for us—but more than anything, it's a reminder of what we're here to do: help organizations use AI in ways that actually serve the people they're built for.

For public sector organizations, this means working with us is now a simpler path. No long procurement detours. Just a real conversation about your needs and how we can help.

We're excited to keep building—with our existing clients, and with new ones we haven't met yet.

If you're a government organization curious about AI, or a business looking for a partner who'll be straight with you about what's possible, we'd love to connect.
